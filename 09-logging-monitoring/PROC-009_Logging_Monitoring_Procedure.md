# 1. Purpose

This procedure defines how AiVenture SRL collects, protects, reviews and maintains logs generated by Artificial Intelligence systems.

The objective is to ensure that AI operations remain traceable, measurable and auditable throughout the system lifecycle.

---

# 2. Scope

This procedure applies to:

- internally developed AI systems;
- externally acquired AI systems;
- production AI systems;
- proof-of-concept AI systems;
- AI infrastructure components;
- monitoring services supporting AI systems.

---

# 3. Responsibilities

## Managing Director

Approves major corrective actions resulting from monitoring activities.

---

## AI Governance Owner

Responsible for:

- coordinating logging activities;
- reviewing monitoring reports;
- maintaining the Logging and Monitoring Register.

---

## AI System Owner

Responsible for:

- implementing logging mechanisms;
- reviewing operational events;
- reporting anomalies.

---

## Compliance Owner

Responsible for:

- verifying log retention;
- supporting audits;
- reviewing compliance evidence.

---

# 4. Procedure

## Step 1 — Identify AI System

Locate the AI system in:

REG-001_AI_System_Inventory_Register.md

---

## Step 2 — Configure Logging

Ensure logs record, where applicable:

- system identifier;
- event timestamp;
- user or service account;
- executed operation;
- system response;
- error messages;
- security events.

---

## Step 3 — Configure Monitoring

Monitor:

- availability;
- performance;
- failures;
- unusual activity;
- configuration changes;
- security alerts.

---

## Step 4 — Review Logs

Review logs:

- routinely;
- after incidents;
- after significant changes;
- before inspections.

Record significant findings.

---

## Step 5 — Update Register

Open:

REG-009_Logging_Monitoring_Register.md

Record:

- AI System;
- logging status;
- monitoring status;
- review date;
- reviewer.

---

## Step 6 — Correct Issues

If deficiencies are identified:

- document the issue;
- investigate the cause;
- implement corrective actions;
- verify effectiveness.

---

## Step 7 — Periodic Review

Review logging and monitoring whenever:

- AI systems change;
- infrastructure changes;
- legislation changes;
- significant incidents occur.

---

# 5. Records

This procedure produces:

- Logging and Monitoring Register;
- Logging and Monitoring Evidence;
- Logging and Monitoring Declaration.
