# ROADMAP

## EU AI Act Compliance Management System™

Repository: RRVI™

Version: 1.0.2

---

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the future evolution plan of the EU AI Act Compliance Management System.

---

# 2. Current Status

Version 1.0.2 includes:

- 10 compliance modules
- 70 controlled documents
- full traceability system
- audit and inspection framework
- governance layer

---

# 3. Short-Term Roadmap (v1.1)

Planned improvements:

- automation of document generation
- template parameterization per company
- AI-assisted compliance validation
- dynamic register generation
- export to PDF / DOCX formats

---

# 4. Mid-Term Roadmap (v2.0)

Planned features:

- SaaS Compliance Platform
- multi-tenant architecture
- compliance scoring engine
- real-time AI monitoring integration
- automated audit reports

---

# 5. Long-Term Roadmap (v3.0)

Planned direction:

- fully autonomous compliance agents
- continuous regulatory mapping
- self-updating compliance systems
- integration with EU regulatory APIs (future)

---

# 6. Research Areas

- AI-driven compliance verification
- semantic traceability graphs
- regulatory change detection systems
- automated audit reasoning systems

---

# 7. Constraints

All future developments must preserve:

- traceability
- evidence integrity
- document control structure
- auditability
- EU AI Act alignment

---

# 8. Versioning Strategy

All roadmap evolution follows semantic versioning:

- PATCH → fixes
- MINOR → new features
- MAJOR → architectural changes

---

# 9. Exit Criteria

v2.0 is considered achieved when:

- system is fully parameterized
- compliance generation is automated
- audit reports are machine-generated
