# 1. Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to establish the principles and requirements for governing data used by Artificial Intelligence systems within AiVenture SRL.

This policy supports compliance with Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 (EU AI Act) by ensuring that data used throughout the AI lifecycle is managed responsibly, securely, accurately and lawfully.

---

# 2. Scope

This policy applies to:

- internally developed AI systems;
- externally acquired AI systems;
- production AI systems;
- proof-of-concept AI systems;
- structured and unstructured data;
- training, validation and operational data where applicable.

---

# 3. Policy Statement

AiVenture SRL shall implement appropriate Data Governance measures for all AI systems.

Data shall be managed to ensure:

- quality;
- integrity;
- availability;
- confidentiality;
- traceability;
- lawful processing.

---

# 4. Data Governance Principles

Data used by AI systems shall be:

- accurate;
- complete;
- relevant;
- up-to-date;
- protected against unauthorized access;
- retained according to company policies;
- processed in accordance with applicable legislation.

---

# 5. Data Quality Objectives

AiVenture SRL shall:

- identify data sources;
- document data flows;
- monitor data quality;
- identify data limitations;
- correct identified errors;
- periodically review data quality.

---

# 6. Roles and Responsibilities

## Managing Director

Approves this policy.

---

## AI Governance Owner

Responsible for:

- coordinating Data Governance;
- reviewing data quality;
- maintaining governance documentation.

---

## Data Owner

Responsible for:

- ensuring data quality;
- approving data usage;
- reporting data issues.

---

## AI System Owner

Responsible for:

- documenting data used by AI systems;
- identifying data limitations;
- supporting reviews.

---

## Compliance Owner

Responsible for:

- monitoring regulatory compliance;
- supporting audits and inspections.

---

# 7. Review Requirements

This policy shall be reviewed:

- annually;
- after significant AI system changes;
- after changes to data sources;
- after regulatory updates;
- before regulatory inspections.

---

# 8. Compliance

Compliance with this policy is mandatory.

Data Governance controls shall be proportionate to the AI system, intended purpose and associated risks.
