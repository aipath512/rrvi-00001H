# 1. Purpose

This Work Instruction provides detailed guidance for identifying, recording, investigating and closing Artificial Intelligence incidents within AiVenture SRL.

The objective is to ensure that every AI-related incident is managed consistently, documented objectively and resolved in a timely manner.

---

# 2. Preconditions

Before processing an incident verify that:

- the AI System exists in the AI System Inventory;
- the incident has been reported;
- sufficient information is available to begin the investigation;
- the AI System Owner has been notified.

---

# 3. Required Information

Collect the following information:

- Incident ID
- Date and Time
- AI System ID
- AI System Name
- Reporter
- Incident Description
- Severity
- Business Impact
- Regulatory Impact
- Corrective Action
- Status
- Closure Date

---

# 4. Work Instructions

## Step 1

Receive the incident report.

Assign a unique Incident ID.

---

## Step 2

Identify the affected AI system.

Open:

REG-001_AI_System_Inventory_Register.md

Locate the corresponding AI System.

---

## Step 3

Classify the incident.

Determine:

- Critical
- High
- Medium
- Low

Assess:

- operational impact;
- business impact;
- compliance impact;
- security impact.

---

## Step 4

Collect Evidence.

Preserve:

- logs;
- screenshots;
- AI prompts;
- AI responses;
- monitoring alerts;
- configuration information;
- witness statements where applicable.

---

## Step 5

Investigate.

Determine:

- root cause;
- affected systems;
- affected data;
- affected users;
- regulatory implications.

---

## Step 6

Implement Corrective Actions.

Document:

- corrective action;
- responsible person;
- completion date;
- verification result.

---

## Step 7

Update Register.

Open:

REG-010_Incident_Reporting_Register.md

Record:

- Incident ID;
- AI System;
- severity;
- status;
- closure date.

---

## Step 8

Close Incident.

Verify:

- corrective actions completed;
- evidence stored;
- management informed;
- documentation updated.

---

# 5. Quality Checks

Verify that:

✓ Incident ID assigned.

✓ AI System identified.

✓ Severity classified.

✓ Evidence preserved.

✓ Root cause documented.

✓ Corrective action completed.

✓ Register updated.

✓ Closure approved.

✓ Supporting evidence retained.
