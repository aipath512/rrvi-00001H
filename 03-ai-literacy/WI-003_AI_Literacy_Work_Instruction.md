# 1. Purpose

This Work Instruction provides detailed guidance for planning, delivering, recording and reviewing AI Literacy activities within AiVenture SRL.

It ensures that personnel using Artificial Intelligence systems possess an appropriate level of knowledge consistent with their responsibilities and the requirements of Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 (EU AI Act).

---

# 2. Preconditions

Before initiating AI literacy activities, verify that:

- personnel requiring AI literacy have been identified;
- applicable AI systems have been identified;
- training objectives have been defined;
- training materials are available.

---

# 3. Required Information

For each participant record:

- Employee Name
- Position
- Department
- AI Systems Used
- Required Literacy Level
- Training Activity
- Trainer
- Training Date
- Completion Status
- Assessment Result
- Next Review Date

---

# 4. Work Instructions

## Step 1

Identify personnel requiring AI literacy.

Include:

- Management
- AI Users
- AI Developers
- AI Governance Personnel
- Technical Staff

---

## Step 2

Determine the required competency level.

Consider:

- job responsibilities;
- AI systems used;
- regulatory obligations;
- operational risks.

---

## Step 3

Conduct the training.

Training may include:

- classroom sessions;
- online courses;
- workshops;
- demonstrations;
- practical exercises;
- self-learning.

---

## Step 4

Assess understanding.

Verify that participants understand:

- AI fundamentals;
- AI capabilities;
- AI limitations;
- AI risks;
- Human Oversight;
- Data Protection;
- Company AI Policies.

---

## Step 5

Record completion.

Update:

REG-003_AI_Literacy_Register.md

Record:

- participant;
- course;
- completion date;
- assessment result;
- reviewer.

---

## Step 6

Collect evidence.

Store supporting evidence in:

EVID-003_AI_Literacy_Evidence.md

Examples:

- attendance lists;
- certificates;
- presentations;
- quizzes;
- screenshots;
- training materials.

---

## Step 7

Review.

Review AI literacy:

- annually;
- after introduction of new AI systems;
- after major regulatory changes;
- after significant AI-related incidents.

---

# 5. Quality Checks

Verify that:

✓ Required personnel have been identified.

✓ Training has been completed.

✓ Assessment results have been recorded.

✓ Evidence has been collected.

✓ Register has been updated.

✓ Review date has been assigned.
